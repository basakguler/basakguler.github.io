Biography:

I received my PhD from the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the Pennsylvania State University, where I was working with Prof. Aylin Yener. After my PhD, I was a postdoctoral scholar in the Ming Hsieh Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Southern California, where I was working jointly with Prof. Salman Avestimehr and Prof. Antonio Ortega.
