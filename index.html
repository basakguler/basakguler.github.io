I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of California, Riverside.

My research is on developing scalable, privacy-preserving, and context-aware communication and information processing frameworks for large-scale distributed networks.

Prospective students:

I am always interested in working with motivated students who would like to pursue research in one or more of the following areas (potentially, the intersection of), communications, learning, information theory, optimization, and signal processing.

My research typically involves both theoretical and implementation components, which requires a strong mathematical background. Familiarity with coding (Python, C++) is a plus but not required. If you are interested in working with me, please send me an e-mail with your CV and research interests.
